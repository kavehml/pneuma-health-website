About Pneuma Health

A welcoming place to begin your healing journey.

Pneuma Health was created to offer mental health care that is compassionate, clinically informed, and responsive to the real needs of the people we serve. We understand that reaching out for support can feel vulnerable, and we aim to make that first step feel clear, respectful, and welcoming.

We believe meaningful therapy begins with trust. Our clinicians work collaboratively with clients to understand their concerns, identify goals, and create a path forward that feels realistic and supportive.

Compassion

We meet each person with empathy, warmth, and care.

Respect

We honor each client's background, identity, beliefs, and pace.

Collaboration

We work with clients, not on them, building treatment around shared goals.

Growth

We support lasting change through insight, skill-building, and practical strategies.

Our Services

Therapy designed to support emotional wellness and meaningful change.

We offer therapy and mental health support for a range of personal, relational, and emotional challenges.

Individual Therapy

One-to-one counselling focused on anxiety, stress, burnout, trauma, depression, self-esteem, and personal growth.

Couples Therapy

Support for communication, conflict, trust, connection, and relationship repair in a structured and compassionate setting.

Family Support

Guidance for families navigating conflict, transitions, boundaries, caregiving stress, and changing family dynamics.

Virtual Therapy

Secure online sessions that make care more convenient and accessible from anywhere in Ontario.
