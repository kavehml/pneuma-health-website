## About Page

### Page Title

About Pneuma Health

### Intro Copy

Pneuma Health was created to offer mental health care that is compassionate, clinically informed, and responsive to the real needs of the people we serve. We understand that reaching out for support can feel vulnerable, and we aim to make that first step feel clear, respectful, and welcoming.

### Our Approach

We believe meaningful therapy begins with trust. Our clinicians work collaboratively with clients to understand their concerns, identify goals, and create a path forward that feels realistic and supportive. We value evidence-based care, emotional safety, and a strong therapeutic relationship.

### Our Values

**Compassion**

We meet each person with empathy, warmth, and care.

**Respect**

We honor each client's background, identity, beliefs, and pace.

**Collaboration**

We work with clients, not on them, building treatment around shared goals.

**Growth**

We support lasting change through insight, skill-building, and practical strategies.

### Closing Copy

Whether you are seeking support for a specific challenge or looking for a space to better understand yourself, Pneuma Health is here to help you move forward with confidence and care.

## Services Page

### Page Title

Our Services

### Intro Copy

We provide therapy services designed to support emotional wellness, healthier relationships, and meaningful personal change. Our care is tailored to individual needs and delivered with warmth, professionalism, and respect.

### Individual Therapy

Individual therapy offers a private space to explore thoughts, emotions, and life experiences with the support of a trained clinician. This service can help with anxiety, depression, trauma, stress, burnout, grief, life transitions, self-esteem, and emotional regulation.

### Couples Therapy

Couples therapy helps partners improve communication, rebuild trust, navigate conflict, and strengthen emotional connection. Sessions focus on practical tools as well as deeper patterns that may be affecting the relationship.

### Family Support

Family-focused sessions can help improve communication, address tension, support major transitions, and create healthier boundaries within the family system.

### Virtual Therapy

Virtual sessions offer a flexible and secure option for clients who prefer remote care. Online therapy can be a practical choice for busy professionals, parents, students, and anyone seeking easier access to support.

## Google Sites Layout Notes

### Home

- Use a full-width banner image for the hero
- Add two call-to-action buttons under the hero text
- Use a three-column layout for the highlights
- Use a four-tile section for services
- End with a simple contact or call-to-action block

### About

- Start with a simple text banner
- Add one image beside the intro copy
- Use a four-column or stacked values section

### Services

- Use stacked text sections or collapsible text blocks
- Add a short intro at the top
- Keep each service description brief and readable

### Team

- Use profile cards with headshots
- Keep bios short
- Add credentials consistently below each name

### Contact

- Add a contact text block
- Add map embed if needed
- Add Jane App button near the top and bottom of the page

## Quick Build Order

1. Create the site and set the site name to `Pneuma Health`
2. Create the five pages in the navigation
3. Paste the copy from each section into the matching Google Sites page
4. Add calming images and your brand colors
5. Link every `Book With Jane App` button to your Jane App booking page
6. Replace all placeholders with your real clinic details
