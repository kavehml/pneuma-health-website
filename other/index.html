Compassion · Access · Dignity

Therapy should be within reach for every person who needs it.

Mental health care is essential—not a luxury. This program is built on the belief that everyone deserves skilled, respectful support, regardless of income. We offer straightforward, affordable sessions and ways to pay kindness forward when you are able.

How easy it is to book with us

Three steps: pick who fits you, choose the package that works, then we connect you with your therapist—clear and straightforward.

  1. Choose your therapist Use the filters below to explore focus areas, then request a match with someone who fits.
  2. Choose your package We help you pick a session plan that fits your needs—options and rates are explained clearly.
  3. Get connected We introduce you to your therapist and set up your first session, online or in person.

Simple, compassionate pricing

We keep pricing transparent so you can plan with less stress. If cost is a barrier, ask us about sliding scale options and the community fund—no one should be turned away without a conversation.

Single session

$99

per 60-minute hour · individual or relational formats as offered

Why this rate

Access first

Priced below many private-market rates to prioritize affordability while supporting fair compensation for clinicians.

Need flexibility?

Talk to us

Email or call—we will do our best to find an option that works, including community funded sessions when funds allow.

Gift therapy to someone you care about

Purchase session credit for a friend or family member. We will coordinate discreetly and respect their privacy. Gift amounts can cover one or more sessions at the standard rate.

Gift cards

Tell us the amount (e.g. one session at $99, or a custom total). You will receive confirmation steps from our team. Payment is arranged securely once details are confirmed—reply by email to begin.
